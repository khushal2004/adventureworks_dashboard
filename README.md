# adventureworks_dashboard
An interactive and comprehensive Power BI business intelligence solution designed to track and analyze the performance of a global manufacturing company. This report provides a deep dive into revenue trends, product performance, and customer demographics to drive data-informed decision-making.

📊 Dashboard Overviews
1. Executive Summary
Focuses on high-level KPIs and overarching business health.

Key Metrics: Tracking total Revenue ($24.9M), Profit ($10.5M), and total Orders (25.2K).

Trend Analysis: Visualizing revenue growth over time with integrated forecasting.

Product Insights: Identifying top-performing categories (Accessories, Bikes, Clothing) and tracking the "Most Returned" items to mitigate loss.

2. Product Detail
A granular view of individual product performance with "What-If" analysis capabilities.

Dynamic Price Adjustment: Includes a price adjustment slider to simulate how changes in pricing impact total vs. adjusted profit.

Performance vs. Target: Gauge charts comparing monthly orders, revenue, and profit against set business goals.

Automated Summaries: Smart narrative integration that provides a natural language summary of product trends.

3. Customer Analysis
Focuses on understanding the audience and purchasing behavior.

Customer Segmentation: Analysis of orders by income level and occupation (Professional, Skilled Manual, Management, etc.).

Top Contributors: A leaderboard of the "Top 100 Customers" by revenue and individual order frequency.

Per-Customer Metrics: Tracking unique customer growth and average revenue per customer.

🛠️ Tech Stack & Skills
Power BI Desktop: Report authoring and dashboard design.

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions): Created complex measures for "Adjusted Profit," "Year-to-Date" calculations, and "Target" comparisons.

Power Query: Data transformation, cleaning, and modeling of the Adventure Works dataset.

Data Modeling: Established a star schema with 1-to-many relationships between sales, products, customers, and territory tables.

💡 Key Business Insights
Revenue Growth: The business saw a significant upward trend starting in mid-2021, peaking in early 2022.

Return Management: While "Tires and Tubes" are the most ordered, "Shorts" show a high return rate, suggesting a need for quality or sizing reviews.

Demographic Targeting: Professional and Skilled Manual workers represent the largest share of the customer base, providing a clear target for marketing campaigns.

🚀 How to View
Download the .pbix file from this repository.

Open using Power BI Desktop.

Interact with the slicers (Date, Product Category, Price Adjustment) to explore the data.
